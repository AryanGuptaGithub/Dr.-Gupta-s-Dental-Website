# Bridal Smile Makeover Guide – Get Wedding-Ready

Your wedding day is the most photographed day of your life. Every smile, every laugh, every candid moment will be captured forever. Is your smile ready?

## Why a Smile Makeover Before Your Wedding?

Your smile is the focal point of your face. In wedding photos, it's what people notice first. A smile makeover can:

- Brighten your teeth by 5-8 shades
- Fix chips, gaps, and misalignment
- Give you the confidence to smile freely
- Make every photo frame-worthy

## Your Wedding Smile Timeline

### 6 Months Before: Start Alignment

If your teeth need straightening, start clear aligners at least 6 months before. This gives enough time for mild to moderate corrections.

### 3 Months Before: Begin Veneers (if needed)

Porcelain veneers require 2-3 weeks from design to delivery. Starting 3 months ahead gives you time to adjust and ensures everything is perfect.

### 1 Month Before: Professional Whitening

Laser whitening gives immediate results. Do this a month before so your smile is at its brightest — and any minor sensitivity has subsided.

### 1 Week Before: Final Polish

A quick cleaning and polish ensures your smile is photo-ready.
